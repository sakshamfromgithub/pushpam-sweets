Our Story

A Family Restaurant Built on Love & Tradition

Since 1995, PUSHPAM Sweets & Restaurant has been serving pure vegetarian food made with love, fresh ingredients, and recipes passed down through three generations. What started as a small sweet shop has grown into a beloved family dining destination, yet our commitment to quality remains unchanged.

Every morning, our kitchen comes alive with the aroma of freshly prepared sweets and spices. We source the finest ingredients, use pure ghee, and follow traditional methods to ensure every bite carries the authentic taste that our customers have come to love and trust.
